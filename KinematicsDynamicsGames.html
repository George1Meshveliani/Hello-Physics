From \(v^2 = u^2 + 2as\): \(64 - 576 = 2a(80)\) so \(a = -3.2\,\text{m/s}^2\). Then \(v = u + at\) gives \(8 = 24 - 3.2t\) → \(t = 5\,\text{s}\).
